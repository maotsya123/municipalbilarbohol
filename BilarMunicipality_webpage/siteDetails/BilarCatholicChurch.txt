Site: Bilar Catholic Church
Details:
Bilar Catholic Church which is dedicated to San Isidro Labrador was constructed in 1870 and is the oldest structure in existence in 
the town. It is situated along the National Road with the front facade of the church, which is the foot of the cruciform, 
facing the road and annexed to it is a stone tower. The church is generally made of massive blocks of stone 
with the upper portions in wood.
